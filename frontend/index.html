Hamilton Golf Card Game

Best Poker Hand Wins the Pot

Rules
  1. If you three-putt you owe $1 to the pot
  2. Any more putts from there on you owe an extra $1, $2 is the most you pay
  3. A one-putt gets you one card and a zero-putt gets you two cards
  4. If you greenie (i.e. closest to the pin on the green in one shot on a par 3), you get $1 from everyone
  5. Longest drive in the fairway is gets you $1 from everyone
  6. No three puts gets you is a card
  7. Best score gets you a card also each player in a tie for first gets a card
  8. Deal cards in order of names on scorecard
